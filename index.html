Current Role and Delivery Profile

My current work combines AI architecture, hands-on engineering, and cross-functional leadership to deliver robust systems end to end.

AI Architecture & System Design

I design agentic and workflow-based AI systems with clear non-functional requirements: scalability, reliability, security, observability, and long-term maintainability. I translate architecture decisions into cloud-native components in Azure or AWS.

Implementation, APIs & Operations

I define API patterns, authentication and authorization flows, CI/CD strategies, dockerization standards, and validation practices for Gen AI and API-based services. I also monitor cost behavior and optimize system design for efficiency.

Evaluation, Guardrails & Quality

I establish evaluation frameworks for LLM and RAG systems using practical metrics and methods such as RAGAS and LLM-as-a-Judge, while addressing hallucinations, drift, security risks, and compliance requirements through guardrails and testing strategies.

Leadership & Client Collaboration

I work directly with clients and internal stakeholders to define roadmaps, manage expectations, identify risks, and align technical decisions with business outcomes. I collaborate with Product, SRE, Data, QA, and Security teams.
